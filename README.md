# Lucky Harvest

## THIS GAME WAS MADE FOR THE VERY SERIOUS JUNIPER GAME JAM 

## AND FOR THE HACKCLUB JAME GAM 

Lucky Harvest is a cozy farm-themed **synergy spinner**. Each round, your farm symbols  are placed randomly on a 4×4 grid I drew (masterpiece drawing ikr). But this isn't pure luck, what is next to each symbol in the grid really **DOES matter a lot** :

_A cow next to crops earns a bonus. A pickaxe doubles adjacent ores. A chicken lays eggs over time. A rat eats whatever prey it finds nearby._

After each round, visit the Shop to draft a new symbol or remove a weak one.  **Survive 12 rounds to win the game!**

### HOW TO PLAY

first here is where you can test it : https://gababouin.itch.io/lucky-harvest

→ Press SPIN to **fill the grid** from your reserve

→ Symbols **earn coins** based on their neighbors

→ **Pay the Rent** at the end of each round or it's game over (the rent is payed automatically from your coins)

→ Visit the Shop to grow and **refine your collection**

→ Reach Round 12 to win!

### TIPS

- A **small and focused reserve** beats a big messy one

- The remove button is as **powerful** as the buy button

- Watch for **chain reactions**, cow produces milk, chicken lays eggs

- High-risk symbols like rat and slime can **pay off big**
